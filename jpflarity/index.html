Welcome to my website

It all starts with you, my reader, and your wonderful brain. Synapses are firing. Optical receptors sort data. Pixels become letters. Words become sentences. Then chunks of text grow into a dangerous thing: ideas.

Who is J.P. Flarity?

About

We live as something more than animals, something less than the divine, imprisoned in a shell of meat until we hit our expiration date, burdened with the ultimate condition of our existence:

the ability to choose.

Why did we do this to ourselves? Was omnipotence so tiring? How does this affect the meaning we inject into our lives? And ultimately, what’s the point?

I write fiction as an attempt to answer the unanswerable questions. Currently, the pile of rejection letters grows into the ionosphere, but I will keep climbing this mountain of skulls, bashing my head against the keyboard until the lock opens.

When I am not writing, I exist as an “apparent human” in the Pacific Northwest, where I humble myself teaching middle school and live in the woods with a herd of elk.
